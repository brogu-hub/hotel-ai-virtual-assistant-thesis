# STR / Revfine: AI Upselling and RevPAR Growth

> **Authors**: STR (hospitality analytics firm), Revfine (hotel revenue management platform)
> **Venue / Year**: Industry research, 2025–2026
> **Link**: Not directly cited — referenced in `LLM_CHATBOT_CRITIRION.md`

## Key claim

AI-driven pricing and upselling agents have generated upward of **10–30% growth in RevPAR** (Revenue Per Available Room) compared to static booking engines.

## Methodology

Industry analysis of hotel performance data from properties that deployed AI upselling tools versus control groups using static booking engines.

## Results

- 10–15% RevPAR growth cited in one reference (conservative)
- 10–30% RevPAR growth cited in another reference (broader range)
- Triggered upsells during amenity inquiries (spa, breakfast) are the most effective pattern

## Relevance to thesis

Provides economic justification for implementing agentic upselling (the "Nudge Node" pattern) in the hotel assistant. Relevant to a thesis discussion of AI business value in hospitality. The current codebase does not implement upselling — this data supports it as a future work recommendation.

## Quotes worth keeping

> "Hotels using 'Agentic Upselling' see a 10–30% increase in direct revenue compared to static booking engines." — Revfine / STR
